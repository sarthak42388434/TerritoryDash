About Territory Conquest

Game Overview

Territory Conquest is an exciting multiplayer territory capture game inspired by Paper.io. Navigate your colored block around the playing field to capture as much territory as possible while avoiding collisions with other players' trails and your own tail.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or touch controls to move
  • Create closed loops to capture territory
  • Avoid hitting trails (yours or others')
  • Dominate the leaderboard!
